Our Mission

VIRTI JAIN PATHSHALA is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering spiritual growth through Jain principles. We strive to create an environment where students can excel academically while developing strong moral values.

Our Vision

To become a leading educational institution that combines modern technology with traditional Jain values, preparing students for success in both worldly and spiritual pursuits.

Our Core Values

Ahimsa (Non-violence)

Promoting peace and harmony in all aspects of life

Satya (Truth)

Encouraging honesty and integrity in all dealings

Asteya (Non-stealing)

Teaching respect for others' property and rights

Brahmacharya (Celibacy)

Fostering self-discipline and self-control

Aparigraha (Non-possession)

Promoting simplicity and contentment

Why Choose Us

Quality Education

Comprehensive curriculum designed for holistic development

Spiritual Growth

Integration of Jain principles in daily learning

Community

Building strong relationships and support networks
